Private Swim Lessons

  • Personalized attention through one-on-one instruction
  • Flexible lesson times that work with YOUR schedule
  • Enjoy the comfort of a non-public pool
  • Guaranteed to learn to swim in under eight lessons

What to expect

Start by booking a time for a 5-10 minute call, so I can learn about your goals for taking swimming lessons, then we'll schedule our first lesson.

Each lesson will run between 45 minutes to an hour, depending on your availability and objectives. No special equipment is necessary; I do recommend purchasing goggles and a rash guard to keep you warm as the evenings can get chilly.

As a beginner, you will learn how to breath, kick, paddle, and float. Within eight lessons, you will be able to swim across the pool independently.

For swimmers with some prior experience, we'll focus on refining your technique to improve your endurance and speed.

About

Hi, my name is Eve — I have been teaching swimming to adults and kids for nearly 10 years! I worked for five summers at the Carol MacPherson Aquatic Center, then one for UCSF. Now I offer private lessons to adults either in the pool at my apartment complex or at my clients’ pools.

I’m also a college senior learning Python, among other skills. I think that learning to code is in many ways similar to learning how to swim: you have to instill good habits from the start, understand the reason why things are done a certain way, and then practice regularly until you’re confident.

I’m excited to share my skill with you.
